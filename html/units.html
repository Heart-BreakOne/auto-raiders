Prioritize your units here

  • Enable priority list on the settings
  • Make sure the unit selectors (common, uncommon, rare and legendary) are enabled accordingly otherwise the priority unit will be ignored
  • Placement of a priority unit will depend on marker and cooldown availability
  • Priority 0: Completely ignores the unit, even if there are no other units available.
  • Priority 1: Highest priority.
  • The higher the number, the lower the priority and the lower the unit will show at the list.
