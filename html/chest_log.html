Reward Summary

% represents the rate at which you obtained that item in a specific slot for a given ChestId. For instance, if a single Amazon scroll in Slot 1 has a percentage of 20, it means that 20% of the chests with that specific ChestId contained a single Amazon scroll in Slot 1.

After you've accumulated a decent amount of log entries, this number will show the approximate chance of getting each loot item.
